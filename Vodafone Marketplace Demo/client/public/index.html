How do I get started on Vodafone Business Marketplace?

1-9 Employees ‚Äì If you have 1 to 9 employees and are an existing Vodafone mobile customer, you are all set! You will be able to login using your usual My Vodafone username and password.

10+ Employees ‚Äì If you have 10 or more employees, you will first need to speak with us. You will get access to the Vodafone Business Marketplace when you purchase your first application from us. We will send you an activation email, which you need to respond to within 15 minutes.

What is Vodafone Business Marketplace?

The Vodafone Business Marketplace is a web-based digital platform that lets you access and manage a number of your business products and services from one simple and secure place.

  • Access and log in to all your Vodafone Business Marketplace purchased products and services from one place
  • Manage your subscriptions from one place (e.g. purchase more licences, upgrade to different editions, amend or cancel your services)
  • Buy other products & services
